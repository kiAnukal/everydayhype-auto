  HOOK: "Here's what you need to know 🌙"
  HEADLINE STYLE: "TRUMP JUST CHANGED THE AI GAME FOREVER"
  WHY IT WORKS: famous name + huge stakes + bold verb

  HEADLINE STYLE: "GOOGLE CEO SUNDAR PICHAI SAYS AI WILL REWRITE HOW SEARCH WORKS"
  WHY IT WORKS: named company + named CEO + a concrete bold prediction

  WHY IT WORKS: ordinary-person hero + real-world stakes + a trendy term ("vibe coding")
